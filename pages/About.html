How We Work

Our platform operates on three core principles: direct relationships, radical transparency, and fair pricing

Direct Partnership

Every farmer on our platform is personally vetted by our team. We visit each farm, verify organic certifications, and establish long-term partnerships.

  • Average 3.2-year partnership duration
  • 95% farmer retention rate
  • $4.8M paid directly to farmers in 2024

Radical Transparency

Every product includes detailed farm information, harvest dates, soil test results, and the farmer's story. No hidden fees or markups.

  • QR codes on every package
  • Real-time harvest tracking
  • Blockchain-verified certifications

Fair Pricing

Our farmers receive 65-75% of the final price (vs. 20-30% in traditional retail), while customers save 15-25% compared to grocery stores.

  • No middleman markups
  • Dynamic pricing based on harvest
  • Volume discounts passed to customers
